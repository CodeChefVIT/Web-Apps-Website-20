‘Functional WebApps’ is a premier CodeChef-VIT workshop which takes you on an in depth journey on how to become a web-developer and build your own websites easily from scratch. Learn from industry experts and get to know about their journey into the world of Web Development. The workshop is broken down into two components: Client-Side Web-Development(Frontend) and Server-Side Web- Development(Backend). Enroll and learn how you can build WebApps from scratch and exactly how they work.
